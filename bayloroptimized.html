Baylor Optimized

2026

An interactive campus routing tool built on Baylor University's real street network. Pick any two campus locations and watch Yen's k-shortest-paths algorithm animate the best route and its top alternatives

Approach

The street network is pulled from OpenStreetMap with OSMnx, snapped to semantic points of interest (buildings, dorms, landmarks), and served by a small Flask + NetworkX API deployed on Render.

Try it

Hover over a white dot to see its name. Click one to set your start, click another to set your destination.
